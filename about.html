About Me

Click to download my engineering focused resume or my development focused resume. (Right Click -> Save Link As)

In 2013, I graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in mechanical engineering. During my junior year, as part of an internship, I moved to Shanghai, China to work for Siemens. For eight months, I was tasked with updating and modifying engineering drawings and led several English classes. After this internship ended I traveled throughout South east Asia for two months. My interest in international development work was born. Upon returning to college, I joined Engineers Without Borders, and worked on a water system in Kolunje, Kenya and also took a second internship with LightAge working on optomechanical systems. Upon graduating, I joined Peace Corps as an environmental health specialist in Panama. During this time, I have worked with several volunteers to educate community members, construct components of water systems, and create technical guides to assist volunteers in project implementation and daily living. I have also allocated time to pursue hobbies, i.e., photography, painting, drawing, web design, and game development.
